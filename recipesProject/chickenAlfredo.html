Description
Chicken Alfredo is a classic Italian-American dish that consists of cooked fettuccine pasta served with a creamy sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese. The dish is typically topped with grilled or sautéed chicken, which is seasoned with garlic, salt, and pepper. Chicken Alfredo is a rich and indulgent dish that is perfect for a special occasion or a comforting meal at home. It pairs well with a variety of side dishes, such as garlic bread or a simple green salad, and can be customized to suit individual tastes by adding vegetables or other ingredients to the sauce.
Ingredients
  • 1 Pound of Fettuccine pasta
  • 2-3 Boneless Chicken breast (grilled or sautéed)
  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Pint of Heavy cream
  • 1 Cup of Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it is al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill or sauté the chicken until it is cooked through, and set it aside.
  3. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté it for about 1 minute until it is fragrant.
  4. Pour the heavy cream into the skillet and stir it into the garlic butter until well combined. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet and stir it in until it is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  6. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips and add them to the skillet. Stir the chicken into the sauce until it is evenly coated.
  7. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss it with the sauce until it is well coated.
  8. Season the Chicken Alfredo with salt and black pepper to taste. You can also add additional Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs like parsley or basil for garnish.
